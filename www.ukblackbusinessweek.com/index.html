Unifying Black Entrepreneurs, Professionals & Allies of the Black Community

Develop. Empower. Inspire. Recharge.

UK Black Business Week is the biggest business event for Black professionals and entrepreneurs in the UK & Europe.

Through a series of 21 inspirational events in October we equip attendees with business insights, routes to success, new skills and knowledge to navigate the world of work. Network with 15,000 like-minded people. Learn from the experiences of 200 speakers. Support Black-owned businesses and help them grow.
