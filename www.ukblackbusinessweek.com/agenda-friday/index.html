AI & Lending: A hands-on workshop

In this interactive session, participants will explore how AI is transforming the lending process in banks and financial institutions. The workshop begins with a brief overview of how lending decisions are traditionally made, followed by a hands-on team activity. Acting as bankers, teams will use AI tools to perform credit analysis on real-world business cases. The session concludes with a group discussion where teams present their findings and collectively decide which company to lend to, highlighting the interpretative and ethical aspects of using AI in financial decision-making. By the end, participants will gain practical insights and experience in integrating AI into lending workflows.

The Bola Sol Show

18:25 - 18:35

Welcome to The Bola Sol Show

18:35 - 19:15

Money & Motherhood Babies, Budgets & the Career Cliff

Lets talk honestly about what motherhood really costs. UK mums earn 4.44 less per hour than dads, and even with government support, nursery fees still hit 70.51 per week. The career cliff is real too, and its steep. Join this candid conversation about how having children, or even wanting them, shapes your career opportunities. Well dig into the balance that so many are trying to strike, and explore how we can better support working women navigating motherhood in todays economy.

19:15 - 19:55

Community vs Capitalism

When did we become so isolated? Capitalism has taught us that hiring help is better than asking each other. That self-sufficiency is success. Maybe thats making us lonelier and poorer. There are many ways in which costs hit harder when you dont have multi-generational wealth and resources to fall back on. So how do we bridge that gap and get community back. Explore how to rebuild lost networks, why we all need to learn to ask for help, and the value of trusted mutual aid.

20:10 - 20:50

Leaving the UK The Great UK Escape

517,000 people left the UK in 2024 – thats 11% more than the year before. Maybe youve thought about it too? Join us as we explore why so many are choosing to go, from cost of living pressures to quality of life concerns. Well look at where people are heading – Dubai, Portugal, Texas – but lets get real about what youre actually signing up for. Dubais zero income tax sounds great until you hear about those debtor laws. Portugals tax perks? Theyve been scaled back. And Texas might not have state income tax, but wait until you see those property tax bills. Most importantly, well talk about what you really leave behind when you decide to go.

20:50 - 21:15

Closing conversation with Bola Sol

To end this live edition of The Bola Sol Show on a high, sit down with Bola herself to hear all about the journey, ask your questions, and receive incredible wisdom.
