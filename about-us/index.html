Our Story

Women in Food & Agribusiness Innovation (WiFAI) exists to give voice and agency to women shaping the future of food. Founded by Nana, whose work has empowered more than 11,000 women across 31 countries, WiFAI brings together founders, farmers, researchers, investors and policymakers to accelerate innovation and economic empowerment.

Our Mission

To unlock opportunities for women (and men) across the food and agribusiness value chain through collaboration, innovation, market access, investment, data, and storytelling.

Our Core Pillars

Voice

Amplifying women's expertise, achievements, and innovations

Agency

Providing tools, networks and capital to influence systems

Innovation

Showcasing agritech, climate solutions, processing, and food systems research

Connection

Building continental and global networks of partners and allies
