About WAW London

Empowering women in agribusiness across the UK, Africa, and beyond

Our Mission

WAW London - Women in Agribusiness Week London 2026 is dedicated to empowering and supporting women in agriculture and agribusiness across the UK, Africa, and beyond. We believe in creating a platform where women can connect, learn, grow, and thrive in the agricultural sector. Our mission is to bring together the entire food & agribusiness women ecosystem, connecting innovators, investors, and leaders to drive sustainable growth and impact.

What We Do

Over seven immersive days from 21st to 27th September 2026, we bring together the UK and Africa — and the world — to learn, connect, invest and celebrate. Through our flagship conferences, seven thematic streams, workshops, masterclasses, and networking events, we equip attendees with business insights, routes to success, new skills and knowledge to navigate the world of agribusiness. Our events feature distinguished speakers, exhibitions, and a grand Women in Agribusiness Awards & Gala Evening.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for women in agriculture and agribusiness globally, fostering a community that drives innovation, creates opportunities, and celebrates achievements in the agricultural sector. We envision a future where women-led agribusinesses thrive, sustainable food systems flourish, and the entire ecosystem benefits from the diverse perspectives and leadership of women.
