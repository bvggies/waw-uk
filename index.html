Women in Agribusiness Week connects the innovators shaping food and agribusiness, the investors fueling sustainable growth, and the leaders driving impact across the UK, Africa, and beyond.

From cutting-edge technologies to sustainable supply chains, this is where ideas meet investment and the future of agribusiness takes root.
