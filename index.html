Unifying Women in Agriculture, Agribusiness Professionals & Allies

Develop. Empower. Inspire. Recharge.

Women In Agric Business Week is the biggest business event for women in agriculture and agribusiness in the UK & Europe.

Through a series of 21 inspirational events in October we equip attendees with business insights, routes to success, new skills and knowledge to navigate the world of work. Network with 15,000 like-minded people. Learn from the experiences of 200 speakers. Support women-owned agribusinesses and help them grow.
